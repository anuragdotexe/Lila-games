# Insights

## Insight 1 — Activity Clustering

Players concentrate heavily in specific regions.

### Evidence

Movement heatmaps show dense clustering in small zones.

### Impact

* low map utilization
* repetitive gameplay

### Recommendation

* redistribute loot
* add alternate routes

---

## Insight 2 — Sparse Human Kill Data

Human kill events are extremely low.

### Evidence

Kill heatmaps appear nearly empty.

### Impact

* misleading combat analysis

### Fix

Include bot combat events.

---

## Insight 3 — Overlapping Hotspots

Movement, loot, and combat overlap in same areas.

### Evidence

Same zones show:

* high movement
* loot
* combat

### Impact

* predictable gameplay
* imbalance

### Recommendation

* spread incentives
* adjust map flow
